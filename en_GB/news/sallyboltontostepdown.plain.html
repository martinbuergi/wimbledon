Sally Bolton OBE To Step Down as AELTC Chief Executive

23 hours ago • 2 min read

The All England Lawn Tennis & Croquet Club has announced that Sally Bolton OBE will step down as Chief Executive following The Championships 2026, concluding six years of leadership that included guiding Wimbledon through the pandemic, delivering major infrastructure investments, and advancing the Club's long-term site expansion plans.
